# Spacing

---

Spacings are mainly used for margins and paddings. But you can also use
them for other purposes like flex gaps, grids, sizes, ...

Spacings in Fractal are based on a 8px scale.

> ##### ℹ️ Info
>
> ---
>
> This simply means that all spacings are multiples of 8px.

You can use any valid 8px multiple but it's recommended to stick to the scale
below to have distinguishable spacings.

Also, this scale is the same in the horizontal and vertical space.

> ##### 💡 Tips
>
> ---
>
> In the examples below, hover your mouse pointer over each block to get the
> token name and the corresponding size in pixel.
